Refunds for Live Virtual and Classroom Training Courses

All the training course cancellations are needed to be updated to Virtiledge over email to support@virtiledge.com and should get acknowledgement, the refunds will be provided as below:

  • Raising refund request within 72 hours of registration: 100% refund
  • If you raise a refund request before 7 days of registration you will get 70% refund.
  • Raising refund request less than 7 days to the workshop: No refund will be applicable.
  • After booking the training, if participants do not appear for the training: No refund will be applicable.
Refunds for E-Learning Training Courses
  • Raise refund request within 48 hours of registration and before accessing 30 minutes of training: 90% refund.
  • After 48 hours of registration and after accessing 30 minutes of training: No refund.
  • Money back guarantee is void if the participant has downloaded the E-Book.
Please Note
  • Refund requests will be processed in 20 business days from the acceptance of email for refund.
  • All refund and cancellations will acquire bank transactional charges from the refund value.
  • In case Virtiledge cancels the training session, 100% refund will be refunded to the participant. As an alternative, the delegate can also choose a different session at no additional cost.
  • No refunds will be made on exam services registration fees.
  • Participant will be paid back the same mode of payment they used to make the training course payment.
