Rescheduling Policy

In case Virtiledge reschedules the training program, the preferences available to the participant are:

  • 100% refund, if the rescheduled training dates do not suit into the participantâ€™s schedule.
  • The participant would have the privilege of rescheduling and attending the training session in future at his/her convenience, at any location, on any date of a scheduled training.
  • The participant, if they so wish, can send a replacement. But, this must be intimated to Virtiledge at least 7 days prior to the training program.

In case, if for some unexpected reasons, participant wishes to reschedule his/her registration to a future training date, a rescheduling fee is charged as mentioned below:

  • If the training rescheduling request is received 7 days prior or more, 10% of training registration fee will be charged.
  • Please note that training rescheduling will be subject to availability of seats.
  • If the training rescheduling request is received within 7 days or less, no training rescheduling will be acceptable. Though, the participant can send a replacement instead.
Please note
  • Virtiledge reserves the right to substitute previously assigned trainer with other qualified trainer, if thought needed.
  • Virtiledge also reserves the right to reschedule or cancel the training session in case of inadequate registrations, or if the trainer cannot join due to unexpected circumstances. You are therefore recommended to consult a Virtiledge consultant prior to making any travel preparations for a training session.
  • In case of a training session cancellation, you will be informed prior to the start date and will be offered full credit to a future training session or a complete refund, promptly.
  • If any participant is not satisfied with the training session, he/she needs to notify Virtiledge within 24 hours of the first training session. Virtiledge will deliver a different training session at no additional cost. Participants should communicate the same to us via email to support@virtiledge.com
  • Please note that training rescheduling will be done only once and is subject to availability of seats.
